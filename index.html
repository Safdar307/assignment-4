Short Info About Dell

Dell Inc. is a renowned American technology company founded by Michael Dell in 1984. Headquartered in Round Rock, Texas, Dell is a global leader in the IT industry, offering a diverse range of hardware, software, and services. Notably, Dell pioneered the direct-to-consumer sales model, revolutionizing the computer industry. The company has expanded its product line to include laptops, desktops, servers, storage solutions, and networking equipment. Dell is also committed to sustainability, with initiatives to reduce its environmental impact. With a history of innovation and customer-focused solutions, Dell remains a prominent player in the tech world.
